Calculating the **SaaS ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)** for a medical AI or Quantum Machine Learning (QML) application is highly lucrative, but it operates very differently from standard "per-user-per-month" B2B software.

In healthcare AI, software isn't usually sold by seat licenses; it is valued based on clinical efficiency, risk reduction, and image processing volume.

The ARR potential for a fully validated, production-grade version of this application scales across different commercial stages:

---

## 1. The Healthcare AI Pricing Models

When bringing an image-based anomaly detection tool to market, providers typically expect one of three structures:

* **Per-Study / Per-Image Pricing (The Standard):** You charge the hospital or diagnostic clinic a flat fee per scan uploaded to your API. Contracts include a minimum annual volume commitment.
* *Rate:* $2.00 to $15.00 per scan (depending on the cancer type and diagnostic complexity).


* **Per-Facility / Annual Platform Fee:** A fixed annual subscription per clinic or hospital department, giving them predictable billing while you absorb the API compute fluctuations.
* *Rate:* $40,000 to $120,000 per hospital site, per year.


* **Hybrid Tiered Model:** A baseline subscription fee (e.g., $2,500/month) that covers up to 500 scans, with a variable overage rate per image beyond that threshold.

## 2. ARR Progression: From Prototype to Enterprise

Because this involves medical diagnostics, your ARR is directly tied to your regulatory and clinical validation milestones.

### Stage 1: The Research & Developer API Phase (Pre-FDA)

Before clinical clearance, the app can only be legally sold for research use, academic studies, or pharmaceutical drug discovery pipelines.

* **Target Customers:** University research labs, biotech startups, clinical trial groups.
* **Pricing:** $500 to $1,500 / month per organization for API access.
* **ARR Potential:** **$30,000 – $150,000 ARR** (Derived from securing 5 to 10 pilot research contracts).

### Stage 2: The Regional Clinic Pilot Phase (Cleared/Validated)

Once the algorithm achieves clinical validation and peer-reviewed benchmark status, you can sell to independent imaging centers and regional private practices.

* **Target Customers:** 20 localized dermatology clinics or private oncology scanning practices.
* **Pricing:** $15,000 / year flat licensing fee per clinic site.
* **ARR Potential:** **$300,000 ARR**.

### Stage 3: The Enterprise Health System Phase (The True Scale)

At this tier, your API integrates directly into hospital PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems) and EHR (Electronic Health Record) workflows.

* **Target Customers:** Mid-sized hospital networks managing heavy imaging volume.
* **Pricing:** $75,000 annual contract value (ACV) per hospital system based on processing ~10,000 automated scans annually.
* **ARR Potential:** **$750,000 – $1.5M+ ARR** (Securing just 10 to 20 enterprise hospital accounts).
